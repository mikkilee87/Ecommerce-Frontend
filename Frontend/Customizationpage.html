How It Works

  1. Submit your design request using the form above.
  2. Our team will review your request and contact you within 48 hours.
  3. Once approved, we'll create a detailed design mockup.
  4. After your approval, we'll hand-paint your custom shoes.
  5. Your one-of-a-kind shoes will be shipped to you in 2-3 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take?

From submission to delivery, the entire process typically takes 3-4 weeks.

Are the paints durable?

Yes! We use high-quality acrylic paints with a protective sealant to ensure your design lasts.

Can I wash my custom shoes?

We recommend spot cleaning only. Machine washing may damage the artwork.

Can I request changes after seeing the mockup?

Yes, we allow up to 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied.
